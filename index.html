Allow me to introduce myself

Welcome, traveller, to my page. Enjoy your brief stay while you get to know me!

My name is Vlad, I’m a scientific Python developer and a student researcher in computational linguistics at the University of Bucharest. You might know me from twitter as @vnfrombucharest or from github as @vene. I’m also on facebook and g+. I am a contributor to the scikit-learn machine learning library for the scientific Python ecosystem. You can transmit your thoughts to me at vladhunter2@vene.ro.

Areas of interest

Corpus pattern analysis in the context of metaphors, similes and other unconventional uses of language, unsupervised word/pattern representations, web corpus building. On the more applied side I have a cool project in mind regarding extracting data from rendered plots. Feel free to e-mail me.

What’s on this site?

You should take a look at the list of my publications. It’s not much, but I try. For now I only have conference posters. The materials for talks that I’ve had the honour of giving are also available.
